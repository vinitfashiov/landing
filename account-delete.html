Account Deletion Request

Welcome to the Account Deletion Page for the Welcome Grocery mobile application.

As required by Google Play policy, users can request deletion of their account and personal data at any time. Your privacy and control over your data are important to us.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

Once we receive your request, we will permanently delete the following:

  • Your Welcome Grocery account
  • Personal details (name, phone, email, address)
  • Order history
  • Saved addresses & preferences
  • Any stored profile information

Note: Some records required for legal or financial compliance (such as invoices) may be retained as allowed by law.
