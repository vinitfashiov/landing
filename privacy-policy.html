Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, mobile number, email address, and password.
  • Profile Details: Delivery addresses, landmark, alternate contact details.
  • Order Information: Items ordered, date and time of orders, payment method (without storing full card details).
  • Support & Communication: Messages, feedback, or other information you provide when you contact us.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, app version.
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the App, including screens viewed, features used, and time spent.
  • Log Data: IP address, access dates and times, app crashes, and system activity.

2.3 Location Information

With your permission, we may collect approximate or precise location data to:

  • Show relevant delivery areas and available services.
  • Provide accurate delivery and address suggestions.

You can control location permissions at any time through your device settings.
